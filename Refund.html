Refund Policy

Nilima Foods provides fresh homemade snacks. Due to the nature of food products, refunds are limited to the following conditions:

Refund Eligibility

  • Wrong product delivered
  • Product damaged during delivery
  • Expired or spoiled items (must be reported within 2 hours of delivery)

Non-Refundable Items

  • Opened or partially consumed food items
  • Orders delivered correctly as per customer request
  • Orders placed by mistake

How to Request Refund

Contact us within 2 hours of delivery with clear photos of the issue. Refunds are processed back to the original payment mode.
